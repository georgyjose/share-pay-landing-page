Data Deletion

Request deletion of your account and personal data.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account profile (name, phone number)
  • Authentication tokens and OTP history
  • Your expense data and comments
  • Your group memberships
  • Your settlement records
  • Your activity history

What May Be Retained

In some cases, certain data may be retained in anonymized form:

  • Group expenses you created: The expense record may remain visible to other group members for their accounting purposes, but your personal information will be replaced with "Deleted User".
  • Settlements involving other users: Other users' records of settlements with you may be retained in anonymized form.
  • Legal obligations: Data required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion.

How to Request Deletion

Option 1: In the App

  1. Open SharePay
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll to Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Via Email

Send an email to privacy@thesharepay.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The phone number associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

Processing Time

  • In-app deletion: Processed immediately. Data removed within 30 days.
  • Email requests: We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days. Data removed within 30 days of verification.

You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been fully deleted.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You cannot recover your data after deletion.
  • If you have unsettled balances, we recommend settling them before deleting your account.
  • After deletion, you can create a new account with the same phone number, but none of your previous data will be available.

Questions?

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at privacy@thesharepay.com or visit our Support page.
